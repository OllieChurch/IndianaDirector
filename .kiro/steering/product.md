# Product Overview

Portfolio website for Indiana Lown-Collins, an award-winning theatre director.

## Purpose
- Showcase theatrical work, credits, and press coverage
- Provide contact information and agent details
- Allow content updates via NetlifyCMS without developer intervention

## Key Features
- Hero section with profile and background image
- About section with biography
- Current/featured shows with image carousels and reviews
- Press & articles section
- Contact form (Netlify Forms integration)
- CV download

## Content Management
Content is managed through NetlifyCMS at `/admin`. Changes are stored in `src/contentManagement/content.json` and automatically deployed via Netlify's continuous build.

## Target Audience
Theatre industry professionals, casting directors, producers, and general public interested in Indiana's work.
